# Business Context

## Company Name
COMTECH

## Product or Service
COMTECH provides ICT solutions and services targeting students. Core services include digital literacy and ICT training, device support (computer, smartphone, and laptop repair), cybersecurity awareness, network setup and Wi-Fi troubleshooting, and tech tutoring. The goal is to help students understand, use, and solve everyday technology challenges so they can learn and work more effectively.

Revenue model: pay-per-service (session-based or job-based billing). No subscription model at launch.

## ICP
**Primary:** Students — secondary school, college, and university level — who need affordable, accessible tech support and digital skills training.

**Key ICP characteristics:**
- Cost-sensitive; prefer affordable, transparent pricing
- Varied technical confidence; benefit from clear, jargon-free communication
- Need hands-on or remote support for devices (laptops, phones, tablets)
- Want to build practical digital skills (office tools, internet safety, basic coding literacy)
- Often face device failures, connectivity issues, or lack of guidance before exams or deadlines

**Secondary (longer-term):** Schools and educational institutions seeking group training packages.

## Mission
Connect, Learn, and Solve through innovation, education, and reliable technology services.

## Go-To-Market Strategy
- **Stage:** Early idea — no clients or operational setup yet.
- **Entry point:** Device support and ICT training for students.
- **GTM approach:** Word-of-mouth, campus presence, and social media (TikTok, Instagram, WhatsApp groups). Affordable pricing to build a reference base quickly.

## Business Model
- Pay-per-service: fixed pricing per repair, support session, or training block.
- Group training packages for classes or student cohorts (future).
- No recurring revenue model at launch.

## Business Classification
- Company Type: B2C ICT Services / EdTech adjacent
- Industry: Information and Communication Technology (ICT)
- User Type: Students (secondary, college, university)

## Company Values
- Simplicity: make complex technology accessible and understandable
- Reliability: deliver on commitments consistently
- Innovation: apply practical, modern solutions to real problems
- Education: build student capability, not just dependency
- Integrity: transparent pricing and honest technical advice

## One Sentence Summary
COMTECH is an early-stage ICT services company that helps students build digital skills, fix their devices, and navigate technology challenges through affordable training and hands-on tech support, operating on a pay-per-service model.
